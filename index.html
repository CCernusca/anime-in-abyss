Judgement Details

Tag weights derived from the top 100 most popular anime on AniList

For each tag seen across the top 100 most popular anime: Frequency is the share of those anime carrying the tag (0–1). Avg. Acceptance is the tag's mean community relevance rank on those anime (0–1). Score is Frequency × Avg. Acceptance — a high score means a tag is both common and strongly relevant among popular anime, i.e. a mainstream signal. Low-score tags are candidate niche indicators.
